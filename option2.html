My Trip to London

Origin

Flew from Lagos international Airport to London on the 22nd of December 2019. It was a 6 hour Journey with no stop overs.

Feeling happy and excited, I picked up a cab from the airpot to the hotel. It was really expensive.

Next Destination

I took a train this time(much cheaper option) to hyde park and had a swell time. It was Christmas and there were many things to do for fun in the park

Next Up..

I decided to go see a live football (soccer) match at Vicarage road, the home of Watford football club. A long journey it was, but I couldn't care less

The atmosphere in the stadium was electrifying, never seen anything like it before. Football is just like a religion in England

During my stay in London, I visited all the Chinese restaurants in London Excel before I eventually left for Amsterdam where I had my Heineken Experience.
