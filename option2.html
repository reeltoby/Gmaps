My Europe Tour

Origin

Flew from Lagos international Aiport to Amsterdam on the 22nd of December 2019. While in Amtserdam, I visited the Heineken Centre for a wonderful Experience.

Next destination

Left Amsterdam on the 24th of December for Copenhagen, Denmark. While in Copehagen, I visted Tivoli Gardens and had a wonderful time

Final Destination

I then left Copehagen for London where I had a splendid time as well. While in London, I visited very many places including Hyde Park, O2 Arena, The Museum and a lot more. The most memorable moment for me was seeing a live football (soccer) game in Watford. I watched my darling soccer team play live, a jaw dropping experience it was for me.
